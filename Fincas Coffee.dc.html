— OUR STORY

Four generations,
one finca.

In 1978, our great-grandfather planted the first rows on a hillside farm in Honduras — red volcanic soil, cloud-forest shade, and creek water that still feeds the trees today. Four generations later, the finca is still ours: same slope, same shade trees, the same family walking the rows before sunrise.

We control the whole journey — the picking, the washing, the roasting, the pour — so what lands in your hands tastes like where it came from. No brokers, no blends of convenience. Just the bean, carried from our hillside to your cup.
